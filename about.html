Our Story

Our Foundation Is Built on Trust

Smooth-Sail Construction Nigeria Limited (S.S.C.N.L.) was founded with one clear mission to raise the standard of construction and fabrication services in Nigeria. Backed by a leadership team with over 22 years of combined industry experience, our foundation is as solid as the structures we build.

Our president, Engr. Jackson, brings two decades of hands on project management expertise, while Engr. Ibade our vice president, is a trusted specialist in structural steel fabrication and welding. Isaiah Jnr, our operations manager, completes the trio with nearly two decades of experience in industrial piping and plumbing.
